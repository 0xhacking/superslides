• Superslides is a full screen slider for jQuery.

    It's designed to be flexible, with very little styling done in the plugin itself. There's some recommended markup and CSS, but you can customize to your heart's content.

    Full documentation is available at Github.

    Download v0.2.4 - 2kb minified

  • It's light on features, but easily configurable.

    Currently, Superslides has a few configurable options: auto play with a delay, slide transition speed, and slide easing (jQueryUI or the jQuery easing plugin required). 

    delay: 5000
play: false
slide_speed: 'normal'
slide_easing: 'linear'
nav_class: 'slides-navigation'
container_class: 'slides-container'

  • Bind to events and do your own thing.

    Superslides triggers a few events that you can bind to (listed in the order they are fired): 

    slides.initialized
slides.animated

    When the window is resized and on first load before slides.initialized: 

    slides.image_adjusted
slides.sized

  • You might even be interested in contributing.

    Fix a bug or add a feature by forking on Github.
